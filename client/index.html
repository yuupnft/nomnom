How to buy?

Are you new to Solana memecoins and don't know how to trade and become a part of the community? Here are some simple steps to get you started!

  • First, you need SOL, the cryptocurrency of the Solana blockchain.
  • You can get SOL on any major exchange, such as Coinbase or Binance.
  • Once you have SOL, the easiest way is to create a Phantom wallet. You can read more about it on the Phantom website.
  • Once you have the wallet, you can send SOL from your Coinbase/Binance account to your Phantom wallet.
  • To buy the awesome NOMNOM coin and become part of the culture, you can use the Jupiter aggregator. Connect your Phantom wallet and just swap SOL for NOMNOM.
  • Happy trading!
